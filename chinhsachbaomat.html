Privacy Policy

At Thinkmay, we care deeply about your privacy.

This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe when you use Thinkmay CloudPC.

1. What we collect

We only collect the essentials to keep the service running smoothly:

  • Google email address – used for authentication via Google OAuth2 (we don't use it for marketing or spam).
  • Basic usage data – general in-app activity, tracked with RybbiRybbit, a privacy-first analytics tool compliant with GDPR.
  • Technical logs – like device type, browser, and error reports (for debugging only, never personal).

2. How we use your data

We use your information to:

  • Log you in and verify your account.
  • Improve system performance and user experience.
  • Process your plan payments and renewals (via Stripe, MoMo, PayOS, or Thesigure).
  • Understand product usage patterns in an anonymous and aggregated way to make Thinkmay better.

3. Data storage & security

Your data is stored on Thinkmay-owned servers hosted.

We don't transfer user data outside the country.

We protect your data through:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • Strict internal access control.
  • Regular system and security audits.

Still, no system is 100% secure — please keep your Google account and device protected.

4. When we share data

We only share data when absolutely necessary:

  • To complete a payment through Stripe, PayOS, MoMo, or Thesigure.
  • To analyze anonymous usage events through RybbiRybbit.

All our partners have their own privacy policies and are contractually bound to use your data only within Thinkmay’s intended scope.

5. Your rights

You can:

  • View or update your account information.
  • Cancel your subscription at any time.

When you cancel, your uploaded files and usage data are deleted. However, your Google email (used for login) remains in our system to prevent duplicate or fraudulent sign-ups.

6. Cookies & tracking

Thinkmay does not use cookies or third-party tracking scripts.

All analytics data we collect is anonymous and non-identifiable.

7. Payments & third-party services

When you make a payment, minimal transaction data is sent securely to our payment partners:

  • Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • MoMo: https://www.momo.vn/chinh-sach-quyen-rieng-tu
  • PayOS: https://payoo.vn/chinh-sach-quyen-rieng-tu.html

We never store credit-card numbers or banking credentials on our servers.

8. Policy updates

We may update this policy as our product or partners change.

If we make major updates, we’ll notify you on our website or in-app.

By continuing to use Thinkmay after the update, you agree to the new version.

9. Contact

For questions, complaints, or notices, contact us via the official Thinkmay Facebook Page:

https://www.facebook.com/thinkonmay
